Adoption Rules
  • A parent has to be minimum 25 years of age to adopt.
  • An adoption petition is filed in court, and upon approval, an adoption order is issued, legally establishing the parent-child relationship.
  • A thorough assessment of the prospective adoptive parents' suitability is conducted through a home study.
